Spatial Econometrics

Zombie Firms in Italian Manufacturing

Replication and expansion of a Bocconi thesis to all 20 Italian regions, 74,726 manufacturing firms over 2016 to 2024. The standard province-by-sector spillover measure is null; a distance-weighted measure at 100 km radius produces a significant 2.9pp drop in healthy-firm investment. XGBoost early-warning model included.

Text Analysis

Pink Tax Classification

Two-stage pipeline classifying gendered products in UK supermarket data and decomposing observed price differences. A gradient-boosting classifier on TF-IDF text features and product colour reaches F1 = 0.79; a downstream regression decomposes the raw price gap into category-mix and within-category components.
